About Me

Hi, I'm Raghav! I'm a graduate student specializing in Computer Vision and Machine Learning at the Georgia Intitute of Technology. Here, I work as a graduate researcher at the Computational Perception Lab and as a graduate teaching assistant in the College of Computing. I obtained my Bachelors in Computer Science from Georgia Tech as well, where my concentrations were Info-Internetworks and Artificial Intelligence.


I'm fascinated by the applications of computer vision and love working on related but fun side projects during my free time or at hackathons! My other interests include football, table-tennis, videography, dance, board games, and Pokémon.
